🍝 Arrabbiata Pasta Recipe

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the penne pasta until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta and keep aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add chopped garlic and sauté until golden.
  5. Add red chili flakes and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Pour in the tomato puree and mix well.
  7. Add salt and black pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss properly.
  9. Garnish with basil leaves and parmesan cheese.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your spicy Arrabbiata pasta!

Cooking Tips

  • Use fresh garlic for better flavor.
  • Adjust chili flakes according to spice preference.
  • Add a splash of pasta water for a silky sauce.
